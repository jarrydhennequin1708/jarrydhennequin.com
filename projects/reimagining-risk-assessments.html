ANZ Bank UX Design Data Visualization

Reimagining Risk Assessments

I helped ANZ Bank transform their risk assessment capabilities by redesigning 16 Business Intelligence dashboards, improving usability and expanding their impact across the organisation.

Challenge

ANZ Bank, a leading financial institution serving over 5 million customers across Australia and New Zealand, sought to enhance their risk assessment capabilities. The goal was to improve 16 existing Business Intelligence (BI) dashboards used by data scientists and analysts for visualising risk data. The project aimed to boost functionality and usability, enabling users to better interrogate data, generate reports, and share insights with colleagues.

Approach

As the lead UX designer and design sprint facilitator, I implemented a comprehensive strategy over a 10-week period:

  1. User Research: Conducted in-depth shadowing sessions with 24 users from Australia and New Zealand to understand their dashboard usage patterns and pain points.
  2. Opportunities: Identified, categorised and prioritised the most impactful opportunities that would be explored through the design sprint.
  3. Design Sprints: Facilitated collaborative workshops, interviews, and user testing sessions to generate innovative solutions.
  4. Prototyping: Developed interactive prototypes to test and refine new features.
  5. Information Architecture (IA) Optimisation: Ran workshops to identify and address user frustrations with navigating between applications.
  6. Design System Development: Created a cohesive design system and component library.

Opportunities

Our design sprints yielded several groundbreaking features:

  • Cross-dataset metric comparison functionality
  • Enhanced filtering capabilities with over 50 options
  • In-app commenting and email sharing of insights
  • New bookmarking feature for quick access to frequently used views

Outcomes and Impact

The redesigned dashboards have transformed risk assessment processes at ANZ:

  • Expanded User Base: From 30 power users to over 300 novice users globally
  • Streamlined Workflows: Improved efficiency in developing new models and producing reports
  • Enhanced Data Interrogation: Users can now dive deeper into risk data and extract more valuable insights
  • Improved Collaboration: New sharing features facilitate better teamwork and knowledge dissemination

This project showcases how thoughtful UX design can significantly impact complex data analysis tools, making them more accessible and valuable to a broader range of users within a large financial institution.
