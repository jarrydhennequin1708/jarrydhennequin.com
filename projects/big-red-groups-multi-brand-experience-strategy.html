Big Red Group Design System UX Lead

Multi-Brand Product Strategy

As UX lead for Big Red Group, I redesigned their gifting experience platforms. The project included creating a multi-brand design system, optimising user flows, and enhancing search functionality. The result was improved user engagement and a scalable foundation for future growth across all BRG brands.

Challenge

Big Red Group (BRG), the parent company for Red Balloon, ExperienceOz, and Adrenaline, sought to improve the overall digital experience across their gifting experience platforms. The key challenges were:

  1. Low conversion rates from product pages to cart, indicating user drop-off.
  2. Lack of clear information about experience availability and details.
  3. Skewed product page views, with 15% of experiences dominating traffic.
  4. Need for a unified design system across multiple brands while maintaining brand individuality.
  5. Potential SEO risks associated with information architecture changes.
  6. High staff turnover requiring robust documentation and communication strategies.

Approach

As the UX lead, I implemented a comprehensive strategy to address these challenges:

  1. User Research: Conducted in-depth analysis of user behaviour and feedback to identify pain points in the customer journey.
  2. Design System Strategy: Developed a flexible design system using a tokens framework to ensure consistency across brands while allowing for differentiation.
  3. Information Architecture Optimisation: Redesigned the website structure to improve navigation and searchability while preserving SEO performance.
  4. Key Flow and Template Design: Created optimised user flows and page templates focusing on the product detail page (PDP) and booking process.
  5. Search and Filter Enhancement: Improved search functionality and filtering options to distribute traffic more evenly across experiences.
  6. Documentation and Communication: Implemented robust documentation practices to ensure knowledge retention despite staff changes.

Opportunities

  1. Availability Calendar Integration: Added clear, upfront availability information on product pages to reduce booking friction.
  2. Enhanced Experience Details: Expanded product descriptions and added day-of-event information to help users make informed decisions.
  3. Dynamic Search Results: Implemented an algorithm to showcase a wider range of experiences in search results.
  4. Multi-Brand Design System: Created a flexible design system that maintains brand consistency while allowing for individual brand expression.
  5. SEO-Friendly Architecture: Developed a new information architecture that improved user experience without compromising SEO performance.
  6. Streamlined Booking Process: Redesigned the booking flow to reduce steps and provide all necessary information upfront.

Outcomes and Impact

The project has yielded significant positive results:

  1. Successful Launch: The new ExperienceOz website was launched in early 2024 with no negative impact on SEO performance.
  2. Improved User Experience: Early indicators suggest increased user engagement and higher conversion rates from product pages to cart.
  3. Scalable Design System: Created a solid foundation for rebuilding Red Balloon and Adrenaline websites, ensuring consistency across all BRG brands.

User Impact:

  • Improved intuitiveness and usability across all BRG platforms
  • Reduced confusion and load times
  • Enhanced accessibility for users with diverse abilities

Business Impact:

  • Maintained brand harmony while allowing for individual brand expression
  • Created a scalable framework to support BRG's growth
  • Enabled faster feature launches and updates across multiple brands

Team Impact:

  • Simplified UI maintenance and management
  • Improved flexibility and adaptability in development
  • Enhanced communication through a shared design language
  • Streamlined onboarding for new team members

This project demonstrates how a comprehensive UX strategy can address complex challenges in a multi-brand e-commerce environment. The design system ensures that BRG's products are user-friendly, profitable, scalable, and flexible.
