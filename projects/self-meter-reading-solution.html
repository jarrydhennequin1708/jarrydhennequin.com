Origin Energy Product Design User Research

Self-Meter Reading Solution

Developed a self-meter reading solution for Origin Energy to reduce disputed bills and costs. Through user-centered design and iterative testing, implemented a photo submission feature. Result: Over 2,000 submissions, $100,000+ saved, and improved customer satisfaction.

Challenge

Origin Energy, Australia's leading electricity, gas, and solar energy retailer, faced a significant problem:

  • Approximately 10% of electricity bills were disputed, requiring meter re-reads by distributors.
  • This issue resulted in a $20 million loss due to associated costs.
  • The company needed an innovative solution to reduce disputes and costs while improving customer satisfaction.

Approach

We implemented a user-centered, data-driven approach to address this challenge:

  1. Problem Exploration: Conducted workshops to uncover known opportunities and pain points.
  2. Collaborative Ideation: Ran sketching exercises with business partners and engineers, generating ideas such as:
    • Using customer-submitted photos with OCR technology
    • Implementing Mechanical Turk for meter reading
    • Developing a voice-driven UI for streamlined data entry
  3. MVP Testing: Created a Formstack-based MVP and sent it to 250 customers to test our assumptions.
  4. Data Analysis: Analysed user preferences, finding that 95% of customers preferred submitting a photo over manual entry.
  5. Iterative Improvement: After identifying issues with photo clarity, we refined instructions and ran another experiment, yielding improved results.

Opportunities

  1. Customer Empowerment: Enabled customers to submit their own meter readings, reducing reliance on distributors.
  2. Photo Submission Feature: Implemented based on strong user preference (95%) from initial testing.
  3. Clear User Instructions: Developed precise guidelines to ensure high-quality, usable meter photos.
  4. Streamlined Process: Created a user-friendly interface for easy submission of meter readings.

Outcomes and Impact

The self-meter reading solution has delivered significant benefits:

  1. Widespread Adoption: Over 2,000 submissions received for the self-meter read feature as of December 2018.
  2. Cost Savings: Origin Energy saved over $100,000 through reduced need for professional meter re-reads.
  3. Improved Customer Experience: Empowered customers with a convenient way to ensure accurate billing.
  4. Reduced Disputes: Lowered the number of bill disputes, improving customer satisfaction and reducing administrative burden.
  5. Data-Driven Decision Making: Validated assumptions through real-world testing, ensuring the solution met actual user needs and preferences.

This project demonstrates how a user-centered, iterative approach can solve complex business problems while improving customer experience. By empowering customers to participate in the meter reading process, Origin Energy not only reduced costs but also increased customer engagement and satisfaction. The success of this initiative paves the way for further innovations in customer service within the energy sector.
