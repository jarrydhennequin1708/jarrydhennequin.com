Barwon Water Service Design Strategy

Utility Experience Strategy

Led a team to optimise Barwon Water's operations through comprehensive service mapping and stakeholder engagement. Identified efficiency opportunities, improved cross-departmental collaboration, and created a modernisation roadmap. Results included significant time savings, enhanced visibility, and improved internal customer satisfaction.

Challenge

Barwon Water, a major water utility provider, faced several operational challenges that were impacting service delivery and internal efficiency:

  1. Lack of documented processes across multiple departments
  2. Siloed operations hindering cross-functional collaboration
  3. Outdated systems and minimal automation
  4. Dissatisfied internal customers due to inefficient service delivery
  5. Limited understanding of experience design processes within the organisation

Approach

As the lead of a small team, we implemented a comprehensive strategy to address these challenges:

  1. Service Mapping: Conducted a thorough mapping of Barwon Water's entire service experience across all departments. Visualised the complete service to provide a holistic view of operations.
  2. Prioritisation: Identified and focused on the most significant problem areas with the highest impact on customer satisfaction and organisational value.
  3. Stakeholder Engagement: Organised a series of remote and in-person workshops to gather detailed information about services. Educated teams about the experience design process and its benefits to ensure buy-in and participation.
  4. Deep Dive Analysis: Conducted an in-depth examination of one prioritised service to expose processes and uncover problems and opportunities.
  5. Opportunity Identification: Explored possibilities for introducing modern applications to streamline project execution, management, customer communication, and document creation.

Deliverables

  1. Holistic Service Visualisation: Created a comprehensive map of Barwon Water's services, providing unprecedented visibility into cross-departmental operations.
  2. Process Documentation: Established clear documentation for previously undocumented processes, creating a foundation for future improvements.
  3. Inter-departmental Collaboration: Facilitated workshops that brought together previously siloed departments, fostering a culture of cooperation.
  4. Modernisation Roadmap: Developed proposals for integrating newer, more efficient applications to replace outdated systems.
  5. Quick-win Optimisations: Identified and implemented immediate process improvements that could be actioned without significant resource investment.

Outcomes and Impact

The project yielded significant positive results:

  1. Efficiency Gains: Identified opportunities to save hours of work through streamlined processes and modern application integration.
  2. Improved Collaboration: Previously siloed managers discovered and implemented quick-win optimisations, leading to better cross-departmental cooperation.
  3. Enhanced Visibility: The holistic service map provided unprecedented insight into Barwon Water's operations, enabling more informed decision-making.
  4. Cultural Shift: Increased understanding and appreciation of experience design processes within the organisation, paving the way for future improvements.
  5. Customer Satisfaction: Early indicators suggest improved internal customer satisfaction due to more efficient service delivery.
  6. Foundation for Future Improvements: The documented processes and identified opportunities created a solid foundation for ongoing optimisation efforts.

This project demonstrates the power of a holistic approach to service design in a complex utility organisation. By mapping the entire service experience, engaging stakeholders across departments, and focusing on high-impact areas, we were able to identify significant opportunities for improvement.

The process not only yielded tangible efficiency gains but also fostered a culture of collaboration and continuous improvement within Barwon Water. This transformation sets a new standard for operational excellence in utility services and showcases the value of experience design in enhancing both internal processes and customer satisfaction.
