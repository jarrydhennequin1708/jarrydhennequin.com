Monash University AI Product 2024

AI-Powered Research Product

Designed an AI-powered research assistant for Monash University, enabling data-driven investment decisions. Created intuitive visualisations and scalable architecture, resulting in improved efficiency and securing funding for wider deployment across Australian institutions.

Challenge

Monash University sought to create an innovative tool that would empower their business development representatives to make more informed decisions about research investment. The key challenges were:

  1. Identifying trending and underfunded research areas in a vast sea of scholarly works.
  2. Creating a user-friendly interface that could visualise complex data relationships.
  3. Developing a tool that could compete in the market and potentially be white-labeled for other institutions.
  4. Ensuring the tool was intuitive enough for business development representatives who may not have deep technical expertise.

Approach

To address these challenges, we implemented a comprehensive, user-centered design process:

  1. Conceptualisation: Collaborated closely with the primary client stakeholder from the initial idea stage to shape the tool's functionality and potential.
  2. Competitor Analysis: Conducted thorough research on existing solutions to benchmark the user experience and identify opportunities for differentiation.
  3. Ideation and Low-Fidelity Prototyping: Generated potential product concepts and created low-fidelity wireframes to define core functionalities.
  4. Usability Testing: Developed clickable prototypes to test feature desirability and usability with potential users.
  5. Visual Design: Created a visually appealing and intuitive interface that effectively communicated complex data relationships.
  6. Development Collaboration: Worked closely with the development team to refine the tool's intricacies and ensure smooth implementation of design concepts.

Opportunities

  1. AI-Driven Data Analysis: Implemented advanced AI algorithms to identify trends and gaps in research funding across various disciplines.
  2. Interactive Data Visualisation: Designed intuitive visual representations of research trends, funding patterns, and potential opportunities.
  3. User-Friendly Interface: Created a streamlined interface that allows non-technical users to easily navigate and interpret complex data.
  4. Customisable Reporting: Developed features that enable users to generate tailored reports for different stakeholders.
  5. Scalable Architecture: Designed the system with white-labeling capabilities in mind, allowing for easy adaptation to other institutions.

Outcomes and Impact

The Monash AI-Powered Research Assistant has achieved significant success:

  1. Successful Initial Deployment: The tool was released to a select group of business development representatives at Monash University.
  2. Immediate Value Recognition: Users reported increased efficiency in identifying promising research areas and potential funding opportunities.
  3. Significant Funding Secured: The tool's success led to substantial funding for white-labeling and rolling out to various institutions across Australia.
  4. Enhanced Decision Making: Business development representatives now have data-driven insights to support their investment recommendations.
  5. Competitive Advantage: Monash University has positioned itself as a leader in research investment strategy, potentially opening new revenue streams through the tool's commercialisation.
  6. Scalability: The white-label potential demonstrates the tool's versatility and value across different academic institutions.

This project showcases how innovative design thinking, combined with cutting-edge AI technology, can transform complex decision-making processes in academic research funding. By closely aligning with user needs and maintaining a focus on scalability, we created a tool that not only solves immediate challenges for Monash University but also has the potential to revolutionise research investment strategies across the academic landscape.
