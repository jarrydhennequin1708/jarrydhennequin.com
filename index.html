About Me

Most companies know they need AI and automation. Few know how to put it in place without wasting months on failed pilots.

I help businesses by using enterprise innovation sprint methods. I've refined these through my work at Accenture, Cognizant, and high-growth startups. They need proven frameworks, not experimental ones. I specialise in turning complex AI strategies into functional automation systems. I use no-code platforms like Make.com and n8n for this.

The result is smart workflows. They cut out manual tasks, lower errors, and grow operations. Best of all, they're ready in weeks, not quarters. Enterprise method, mid-market execution, measurable outcomes.

Melbourne-based, serving clients globally.
